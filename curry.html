Perfect Japanese Curry


Description

Japanese curry is unlike Indian curry or any other curry you've tasted. Using the perfect balance of ingredients, one can make a delicious japanese curry.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb beef
  • 2 onions
  • 2 carrots
  • 3 potatoes
  • S&B Golden Curry Sauce Mix
  • 6 cups of water
  • 4 tbsp cooking oil

Directions

  1. Heat oil and stir-fry meat and onions in oil in a large frying pan for approximately 3 minutes.
  2. Add carrots, potatoes, and other vegetables
  3. Put the cooked meat and vegetables into a large pot. Add water and bring to boil.
  4. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until meat is tender for approximately 10 minutes
  5. Break S&B Golden Curry Sauce Mix into pieces and add them to the pot.
  6. Stir until completely melted. Simmer for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
  7. Serve hot over rice and enjoy!
